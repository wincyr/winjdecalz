Technology Trends Gallery

E-Learning

With eLearning, educational content is delivered to learners through computers, laptops, tablets, or smartphones.

Block Chain

Blockchain technology is used in Massive Open Online Courses (MOOCs) and ePortfolios to verify skills and knowledge.

Gamification

The adoption of gamification is most popular in the K-12 education sector. It’s because kids are quickly engaged in gaming videos or getting higher scores in a game.

Data-Driven Healthcare

Healthcare’s big data market is expected to reach nearly $70 billion by 2025, according to Bain, a consultancy firm.

Telemedicine

During the Covid-19 epidemic, telemedicine advanced significantly. In January 2020, an estimated 24% of healthcare organizations have a telehealth program in place.

Nanomedicine

Nanomedicine is the medical application of nanotechnology, the technology that operates on the atomic, molecular, or supramolecular scale.

Artificial Intelligence meets IoT

The integration of sophisticated technologies into a “Artificial IoT” primarily lowers deviations from the ideal in the manufacturing technique, ensuring great performance, lower costs, and less waste.

Enhanced Role of Data Analytics

Data analytics plays a crucial role in well-organized and effectual business management to make a significant decision based on a detailed analysis of the gathered data.

High-speed 5G IoT - 2021 Update

It enables faster, more stable and more secure connectivity that’s advancing everything from self-driving vehicles to smart grids for renewable energy.
